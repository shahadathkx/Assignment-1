Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about DevConf 2026? We've got answers.

Can I upgrade my ticket later?

Yes! You can upgrade from a Standard ticket to a Pro or Team pass at any time before August 15, 2026, simply by paying the pricing difference through your account portal.

Are group or corporate discounts available?

Absolutely. Our "Team" package is heavily discounted for up to 10 people. If you have a larger team or require custom corporate invoicing, please use the "Contact Us" option in the pricing table.

Will the conference sessions be recorded?

Yes. Standard pass holders get digital recording access for 30 days post-event. Pro and Team pass holders receive lifetime streaming access to all technical talks and workshops.

What is your refund policy?

We offer full refunds up to 30 days before the conference start date. Within 30 days of the event, tickets can be transferred to a colleague free of charge, but cannot be refunded.
